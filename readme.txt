Effortlessly customize your WooCommerce shop with sort toggles and min/max product limits for seamless control and enhanced shopping experiences.

== Description ==

Take full control of your WooCommerce store with shop page sort toggling and min max quantity plugins. Easily customize sorting options on shop pages and set precise min/max quantity limits for products. Simplify your store's interface, enhance the shopping experience, and maintain inventory control with user-friendly settings—no coding required!

Key Features:

* Hide WooCommerce sorting dropdown before, after, or on both sides of the shop loop.
* Works seamlessly on shop, category, and tag archive pages.
* Lightweight and optimized for performance.
* Simple, clean, and intuitive settings page for easy configuration.
* Fully compatible with most WordPress themes and WooCommerce versions.
* Enhance your store’s design and improve the shopping experience by using Eshop Sort Toggling to take control of your WooCommerce shop page layout.
* Easily set minimum and maximum quantity limits for products.
* Automatically validate and adjust quantities in real-time, preventing users from adding quantities outside the defined range.
* Display user-friendly error messages when quantities exceed or fall below the specified limits.
* Manage and adjust quantity limits directly from the WordPress admin panel.
* Helps maintain stock levels and prevents overselling or under stocking of products.
* Ensures a smooth shopping experience by preventing checkout disruptions due to invalid quantities.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the plugin files to the `/wp-content/plugins/plugin-name` directory, or install the plugin through the WordPress plugins screen directly.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' screen in WordPress.
3. Click "Store Essentials -> Sorting Toggle" from  left menu.
4. From the checkbox check any option as you want and click "save changes" button.
5. Click "Store Essentials -> Min Max Quantity" from  left menu.
6. In the form set minimum value and maximum and click "save changes" button.
